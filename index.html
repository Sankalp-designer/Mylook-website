"Our goal is to focus on Women Hygiene at economical rates by providing sanitary pad vending machines for availability of Sanitary Napkins at the right time - at right place along with incinerators to dispose them after use. Our machines provide the convenience of on-site vending without the need for stock storage and without adding to the workload of in-house personnel. Our range of coin operated vending machines offers washroom users an extensive range of small pack convenience products suitable for female washrooms. We are associated with government, private and NGOs. Our awareness campaigns include donating sanitary napkins and educating the slum area girls/women about them. These vending machines are easy to install anywhere like a dispenser. It is manufactured to international standards with a high rate of reliability built in and proven through performance.Users have the reassurance of trusted, high quality products and the vending machines are made from sturdy ABS plastic or metal construction which makes them vandal resistant. All of our vending machines have reliable coin mechanisms"
